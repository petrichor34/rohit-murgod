My name is Rohit Murgod. I am fortunate to live and work on the beautiful Sunshine Coast - the traditional lands of the Kabi Kabi and Jinibara peoples.

I currently work as a physiotherapist in musculoskeletal, sport and occupational settings.

My interests at present revolve around learning of critical theory, decolonial theory, emergent and phenomenological theory in the role of pain. I also value objective, empirical studies and their role in helping people with pain/injury, but am becoming increasingly aware of their pitfalls.

Spreading knowledge protected by power and prestige to the masses is one way I think I'm contributing to this cause. I hope this helps.
